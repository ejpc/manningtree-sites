Welcome to Penelope's

Tucked away on Stour Street in the heart of Manningtree, Penelope's is the warm, welcoming spot for a slice of homemade cake, a fresh breakfast or lunch, and a really good cup of coffee. Pop in, take a seat, and let us look after you.
